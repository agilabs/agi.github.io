Artificial General Intelligence Lab.

WELCOME

Our group aims to develop Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) technologies. We research not only cutting-edge AI algorithms in the classical AI fields like computer vision and natural language processing, but also study future AI technologies, especially like incremental learning, continual (lifelong) learning, unsupervised representation learning, and memory networks. We have interested in the various research topics including but not limited to:

  1. Continual Learning for Artificial General Intelligence
    1. Online incremental and few-shot learning
    2. Continual unsupervised domain adaptation
    3. Generative models: Lifelong GAN, designing GAN encoder for image manipulation
    4. Lifelong language learning and its applications: question answering, goal-oriented dialogues, text classification, language generation, etc.
    5. Universal continual learning for multi-domain tasks

  2. Memory Networks for Autonomous Intelligent Agent
    1. Advanced developmental memory networks
    2. Memory-based personalized recommendation systems

  3. Medical AI
    1. Anomaly detection of Arrhythmia using electrocardiogram (ECG)

RECRUITMENT

We are looking for graduate students to research together in Artificial General Intelligence Laboratory (AGI Lab.) at Computer Science and Engineering, Kyung Hee University. In our lab, we are interested in developing multi-modal AI algorithms associated with computer vision and natural language processing, studing continual learning techniques of modern deep learning algorithms, and medical AI. If you are interested in the above research fields, please contact Prof. Gyeong-Moon Park (gmpark@khu.ac.kr).
